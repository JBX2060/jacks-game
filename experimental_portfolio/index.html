What do I do?

Uhm... I already said that. I'm a web developer, computer programmer, and artist. Oh, right, you want me to be specific... hmmm...

My main interests within those three aforementioned areas include games and mathematical art. I often design fractals such as tree fractals, or the Mandelbrot Set. I also like to code cellular automata and other simulations which display interesting behavior. As for games, I've made a few large projects, such as one where you control a robot who has to escape a laser factory, and another where you build a city out of tanks from the game Diep.io, and defend them against enemies.
